Welcome to the Internal Medicine Residency Program at Yekatit 12 Hospital Medical College. Our program is dedicated to providing comprehensive and rigorous training to aspiring physicians in the field of internal medicine. We strive to produce competent and compassionate internists who are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to deliver high-quality patient care.

Program Overview: Our residency program is designed to provide a well-rounded educational experience that encompasses both clinical training and academic curriculum. The program duration is typically three years, during which residents work closely with experienced faculty members and gain hands-on experience in various aspects of internal medicine.

Clinical Training: Residents rotate through different medical subspecialties, including cardiology, gastroenterology, pulmonology, nephrology, infectious diseases, and more. These rotations take place in our state-of-the-art facilities, where residents have the opportunity to encounter a diverse patient population and manage a wide range of medical conditions. Under the guidance of our experienced faculty, residents develop clinical expertise, refine their diagnostic and therapeutic skills, and learn to provide comprehensive patient care.

Research Opportunities: We believe in the importance of research in advancing medical knowledge and improving patient care. Our residency program provides residents with opportunities to engage in research projects under the mentorship of faculty members. We support and encourage residents to present their research findings at local and national conferences, fostering their professional development and contributing to the advancement of medical science.

Multidisciplinary Collaboration: We foster a collaborative environment where residents work closely with other healthcare professionals, including specialists, nurses, and allied health staff. Our program encourages interdisciplinary teamwork, ensuring a comprehensive and holistic approach to patient care. Residents actively participate in interdisciplinary rounds, tumor boards, and patient management meetings, gaining valuable insights into collaborative healthcare delivery.

Wellness and Support: We prioritize the well-being of our residents and provide a supportive environment throughout their training. We offer mentorship, counseling services, and wellness programs to promote work-life balance and resilience. We believe that nurturing the personal and professional growth of our residents is essential in preparing them for successful careers in internal medicine.

Graduate Medical Education: Upon successful completion of our residency program, residents are eligible to take the certification examination in internal medicine and become board-certified internists. Our program also prepares residents for fellowship training or for a career in primary care, hospital medicine, or academic medicine.

We are proud of our Internal Medicine Residency Program at Yekatit 12 Hospital Medical College and invite aspiring physicians to join us on this rewarding journey. Through our comprehensive training, supportive environment, and commitment to excellence, we aim to shape competent and compassionate internists who will make a positive impact on patient care and the field of internal medicine.

We are proud of our Pediatrics and Child Health Residency Program at Yekatit 12 Hospital Medical College. We invite dedicated and compassionate individuals to join us on this rewarding journey of becoming skilled pediatricians who will make a positive impact on the well-being of children and their families.

Welcome to the Maxillofacial Surgery Residency Program at Yekatit 12 Hospital Medical College. Our program is dedicated to training highly skilled and proficient surgeons in the specialized field of maxillofacial surgery. We offer a comprehensive and rigorous training curriculum that prepares residents to provide exceptional care in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of conditions affecting the face, jaws, and related structures.

Program Overview: Our residency program provides residents with a well-rounded education in maxillofacial surgery. The program typically spans four years, during which residents gain extensive clinical experience, develop surgical proficiency, and acquire a deep understanding of the complex anatomy and pathology of the maxillofacial region. Our goal is to produce competent and compassionate maxillofacial surgeons capable of providing comprehensive care to patients with diverse craniofacial conditions.

Clinical Training: Residents rotate through various clinical settings, including the oral and maxillofacial surgery department, operating rooms, outpatient clinics, and trauma centers. Under the guidance of experienced faculty members, residents actively participate in the evaluation, diagnosis, and surgical management of patients with congenital anomalies, trauma-related injuries, infections, tumors, temporomandibular joint disorders, and other maxillofacial conditions. They also gain experience in dental implantology, orthognathic surgery, cleft lip and palate repair, reconstructive surgery, and facial aesthetic procedures.

Surgical Skills Development: Our program emphasizes hands-on surgical training to develop residents' technical skills and surgical expertise. Residents receive comprehensive training in a wide range of maxillofacial surgical procedures, including dentoalveolar surgery, orthognathic surgery, facial trauma reconstruction, bone grafting, facial rejuvenation, and tumor resection. They have access to state-of-the-art surgical facilities and advanced technologies to enhance their surgical capabilities.

Multidisciplinary Collaboration: Maxillofacial surgery often requires collaboration with various specialties, including orthodontics, otolaryngology, plastic surgery, and neurosurgery. Our residents work closely with multidisciplinary teams to provide comprehensive and coordinated care to patients. They participate in interdisciplinary conferences, tumor boards, and case discussions to develop a holistic approach to patient management.

Didactic Curriculum: Our residency program offers a comprehensive didactic curriculum that covers key topics in maxillofacial surgery. Residents participate in regular conferences, lectures, case discussions, journal clubs, and multidisciplinary meetings. These educational sessions cover areas such as craniofacial anatomy, maxillofacial trauma, oral pathology, anesthesia, implantology, orthognathic surgery, and facial aesthetics. Our program emphasizes evidence-based practice and provides residents with the knowledge and critical thinking skills necessary for sound clinical decision-making.

Research and Scholarly Activities: We encourage residents to engage in research and scholarly activities throughout their training. Our program provides opportunities to participate in clinical research projects, present at national and international conferences, and publish in scientific journals. Residents have access to research mentors, resources, and a supportive research environment to foster their academic growth.

Continuing Education: We believe in the importance of lifelong learning and professional development. Our program supports residents in attending national and international conferences, workshops, and courses to stay updated with the latest advancements in maxillofacial surgery. We encourage residents to pursue fellowship opportunities and advanced training to further enhance their skills and expertise.

Certification and Career Pathways: Upon successful completion of our residency program, residents are eligible to take the certification examination in oral and maxillofacial surgery and become board-certified specialists. Graduates of our program have pursued various career pathways, including private practice, academic positions, research positions, and leadership roles within the field of maxillofacial surgery.

We are proud of our Maxillofacial Surgery Residency Program at Yekatit 12 Hospital Medical College. We invite dedicated and passionate individuals to join us on this exciting and rewarding journey of becoming skilled maxillofacial surgeons who provide comprehensive care and improve the lives of patients with craniofacial conditions.
