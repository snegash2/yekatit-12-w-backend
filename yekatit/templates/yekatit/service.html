ECG Testing: We offer a wide range of ECG tests to assess the electrical activity of the heart and detect abnormalities. An ECG records the electrical signals generated by the heart as it beats, providing valuable information about the heart's rhythm, rate, and overall cardiac health. Advanced Equipment: Our ECG department is equipped with advanced ECG machines that ensure high-quality signal acquisition and accurate interpretation.
